Unit 14 - Installing Computer Hardware

Health and Safety Poster

I created a poster on health and safety measures when operating/repairing computers. This poster highlights essential safety protocols including anti-static precautions, proper handling of components, and workspace safety guidelines to prevent injury and equipment damage.

Installing and Maintaining Hardware

I created a detailed PowerPoint presentation for installing and maintaining hardware. This presentation covers step-by-step action plan for hardware installation, tools used during installtion, and recommended hardware components for upgrade.

Practical Hardware Removal (P4)

For P4, me and Tom worked together and we learnt how to remove hard drive from the computer. This hands-on experience taught us proper disconnection procedures, how to remove old hardware form the computer, and new hardware installation.

Computer Update Procedures

In Unit 14, I explained the steps that are recommended to take prior to updating the computer. These include backing up important data, checking system compatibility, ensuring stable power supply, and creating system restore points to prevent data loss during updates.
